Your kerberos authentication failed or your account has no access to the Errata Tool

Access to the Errata Tool is limited since it contains sensitive information about embargoed security issues.

If you work in Quality Engineering, Development, Security Response, Release Engineering, Content Services, or GSS, you can request access to the Errata Tool.

To request access, please send an email:

  • addressed to errata-accounts@redhat.com
  • cc'ed to your functional manager[1]
  • mentioning your name and kerberos login, preferably in the subject[2]
  • stating the reason you need access

[1] If you aren't sure who your functional manager is, check the organizational chart.

[2] An example subject line: "Requesting Errata Tool access for <Your Name> (user <yourusername>)"
